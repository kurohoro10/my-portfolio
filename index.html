Process

How I work.

A practical, business-first development process — no hand-waving, no surprises.

  1. 01

    Understand business goals

    I start by understanding what problem you're trying to solve — not just what you want built. This shapes every technical decision that follows.

  2. 02

    Plan architecture and user flow

    Before writing a line of code, I map out the structure — data flow, user paths, component breakdown — so the build is predictable and scalable.

  3. 03

    Build responsive and scalable solutions

    I write clean, maintainable code with accessibility and responsiveness baked in — not bolted on at the end.

  4. 04

    Optimize for performance and SEO

    Fast load times, semantic HTML, proper heading structure, and keyboard accessibility — engineering maturity that clients and search engines both notice.

  5. 05

    Deploy and maintain

    I see projects through to launch and beyond — clear handoffs, documentation, and ongoing support when needed.
